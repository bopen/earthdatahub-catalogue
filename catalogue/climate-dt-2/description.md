The Digital Twin for Climate Change Adaptation (Climate DT) is the first-ever effort to operationalize the production of global, multi-decadal climate projections. It leverages the world-class supercomputing facilities of the EuroHPC Joint Undertaking alongside leading European climate models.

This collection features datasets derived from second-generation Climate DT simulations, offering significantly enhanced simulation quality and an expanded data portfolio with respect to the previus generation.

Please refer to the specific datasets for detailed information regarding extent, resolution, updates, and access rights.
