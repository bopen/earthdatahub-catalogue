The Destination Earth Digital Twin for Climate Change Adaptation (Climate DT) is the first-ever effort to operationalize the production of global, multi-decadal climate projections. It leverages the world-class supercomputing facilities of the EuroHPC Joint Undertaking alongside leading European climate models.

Climate DT supports adaptation activities at the specific scales where climate change impacts are most acutely felt.

This collection features datasets derived from second-generation Climate DT simulations. These were run using upgraded climate models that offer significantly enhanced simulation quality and an expanded data portfolio.

Please refer to the specific datasets for detailed information regarding extent, resolution, updates, and access rights.
