The Sentinel-2 mission is a constellation of two polar-orbiting satellites, performing multispectral imaging. This dataset is derived from the Level-3A products produced by the European Space Agency.

They are presented as a Zarr v3 archive optimised for time analysis.

At the moment it is available a sample dataset for [UTM zone 31 and relative orbit 51](https://earthdatahub.destine.eu/collections/sentinel-2-l2a/datasets/Z31N-O51)
